EVENT DESCRIPTION

QUANTUM CRAFT

AI Image Generation

Embark on an exhilarating journey of knowledge and creativity as teams delve into the realms of code snippets. With just 20 minutes for 20 questions, teams race against the clock, unlocking keywords with each correct choice. The stakes rise as these keywords become the building blocks for an imaginative prompt, transformed into a visual masterpiece using the cutting-edge Adobe Firefly AI model. Be prepared for a thrilling fusion of intellect and innovation, where every decision shapes the path to victory in this dynamic competition.

Rule for Quantum Crafts

Duration: 25 minutes
No. of Rounds: 1
Max No. of Participants in a Team: 3 members
Rules of the Event:
 • Each team tackles 20 multiple-choice output questions related to DSA, Python (basic) and OS, with each option linked to a keyword.
 • Teams have 20 minutes to solve 20 questions, receiving the correct keyword upon correct selection and a wrong keyword upon wrong selection.
 • After all questions, teams get 5 minutes to engineer a prompt using the collected keywords and generate an image with the Adobe Firefly AI model.
 • If a team fails to select an option, they proceed to the next question without the associated keyword.
 • In the absence of a correct image, marks are awarded based on the number of correct answers in the MCQ.
 • In case of a tie in correct answers, the team with the prompt and result closest to the original receives more marks.
 • Participants can only use the provided Adobe Firefly AI model after answering all 20 questions.
-The decisions of the judges and organizers are final.
Tie Breaker Round (if needed):
In case of a tie in correct answers, the team with the prompt and result most closely matching the original receives higher marks.
Judgement Criteria:
 • The team generating the image closest to the one the organizers generated wins.
 • The organizers will generate an image with the keyword.
 • In the absence of a correct image, marks are allocated based on the number of correct answers in the MCQ.
AI Model:
Adobe Firefly (Version: Firefly Image 2)
Note: team should have an Adobe account with 25 free credits for Firefly and use the web app on the devices provided to them.
