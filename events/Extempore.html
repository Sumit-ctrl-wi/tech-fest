    • Rules for the Event

    FOR CANDIDATES

     Participant has to speak only in English.
     The maximum time limit to think over the topic will be 1 minute.
     He/ She will take time for speech will be 3 minutes.
     The participant will be allowed to select the topic once only.
     Participants will not be allowed to take any paper or material with him or her during the speech.
     The contestants are not allowed to have any gadgets.
     A timer accessible to the contestant, judges and audience shall be provided.
     A bunch of the topics will be given to select anyone randomly.

    Judging Criteria:

     Judges will be looking for clarity of thought, logical organization, sincerity, and the effective use of plain English.
     In the case of a tie, the winner will be decided by the panel of judges by mutual discussion.
     Participants will be judged on the following criteria --

    1. Approach/Departure
    2. Appearance
    3. Poise/Self-confidence
    4. Voice projection/Inflection
    5. Diction/Enunciation
    6. Facial Expressions
    7. Gestures
    8. Eye Contact
    9. Enthusiasm
    10. Persuasiveness
    11. Clear presentation of ideas
    12. Worthwhile Message
